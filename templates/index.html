Study items are organized into lists and divided by "worlds".

Practice your declarative knowledge (recall of facts) by selecting the "Definitions flashards" from the top of the list.

Practice your procedural knowledge (skills and strategies) by selecting any other topic on the list and solving the question. Check your answer by clicking on "check answer" or get a tutorial video when you click on "hint". To get a new randomly generated question, click on the "new" button in the top right corner of the flash card.

You may need to scroll to see all the available topics in the lists.
